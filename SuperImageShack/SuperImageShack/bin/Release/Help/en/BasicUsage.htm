Taking Screenshots

You can take full desktop screenshot or just the active window. Super ImageShack gives the user diferent ways to take sreenshots:

  • Go to Take menu and click Desktop or Active window
  • Right click on the system tray icon, go to the Take menu and click Desktop or Active window
  • Press F7 (Desktop) or F8 (Active Window) to take a screenshot while ImageShack is minimized. (F7 and F8 are the default keys, click here to see how to change it)
